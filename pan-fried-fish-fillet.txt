Ingridients
-2 eggs
-bread crumbs
-1/2 teaspoon sea salt
-1 cup flour
-1kg fish fillet

Instructions

1.Add the salt into the flour and mix thouroughly
2.Prepare the egg mixture and pour the bread crumbs into a plate
3.Set the bowls in eggs,flour,bread crumbs orderr
4.Chop the fillet into small pieces
5.Add oil into the pan and preheat it for 2 minutes
6.Dip each piece into each of the bowl ensuring an even cover and wipping out any excess
7.Fry the fillet for a few minutes one side at a time for about 3 minutes for each side or till it turns brown
8.Serve and enjoy while hot
